What Is Consulting?

Consultants Are Expected To Be:

  • Knowledgeable about the topic/client problems
  • Well-connected within the industry
  • Have a reputation
  • Effective Communicators

And Work With Clients To:

  • Increase Revenue
  • Reduce Costs
  • Clarify Strategic Direction
  • Advice on Hiring and Release Employees
  • Improve Business Operations

  • Expertise

  • Time and Resource Constraints

  • Diverse Experience
